## Anti-Patterns Verdict

## Overall Impression

The design vocabulary is real. The panel mechanic, grid line system, and contact heading are portfolio-quality decisions. The problem: the most credible content lives behind optional clicks, and the work gallery ends in two visually dishonest cards. Good design deserves better content.
